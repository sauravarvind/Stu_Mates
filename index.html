ABOUT US

We are students from various colleges joining our hands to run this student community called stu_mates . It is an initiative to get ourselves improved and become an eligible resident to lead our society. Though we are budding students, we are eligible to prove ourselves . We are learning within us and also gather experience from experts. Our ultimate aim is to learn from the experience .


"EXPERIENCE IS THE BEST TEACHER"

This community is significantly for individuals who seek knowledge and to uncover new ideas . Stu_mates includes live speakers, recorded talks and are organised independently.
